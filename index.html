Antoni Gaudí

Was a Spanish architect known as the greatest exponent of Catalan Modernism. Gaudí's works have a highly individualized, one-of-a-kind style. Most are located in Barcelona, including his main work,the church of the Sagrada Família. 25 June 1852 – 10 June 1926

How to do Gaudi in a day!

There are landmark's you must see when in Barcelona and for Gaudi fans this is the right place to be Save time with skip-the-line tickets booked ahead of your trip, get an early start, and take in views Catalan capital with a tour of the cathedral’s towers. Then, head to nearby Park Güell, another Gaudí masterpiece, for more city views and quirky, colorful tile work. It’s possible to pack in even more of the architect’s work with a Gaudí-themed tour that includes entrance to both Sagrada Familia and Park Güell, plus a walk down Passeig de Gracia to see Casa Batlló and La Pedrera from the outside.
